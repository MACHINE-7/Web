ABOUT AMD RYZEN

AMD Ryzen is a line of processors developed by AMD (Advanced Micro Devices), designed for desktops, laptops, and servers. Ryzen processors are known for offering strong performance and value, especially in multi-core applications. They use the Zen microarchitecture, which has undergone several iterations.

Some Popular Processors From AMD Ryzen

RYZEN 5 3600: A mid-range processor with 6 cores and 12 threads, known for its excellent performance in gaming and productivity tasks. - [Price: Rs.10,000]

RYZEN 7 3700X: A higher-end option with 8 cores and 16 threads, providing better multitasking capabilities and performance for content creation. - [Price: Rs.20,000]

RYZEN 9 3900X: A high-end processor featuring 12 cores and 24 threads, suitable for demanding applications like video editing, 3D rendering, and gaming at high settings. - [Price: Rs.43,000]

RYZEN 9 5950X: Part of the Ryzen 5000 series, this processor boasts 16 cores and 32 threads, offering top-tier performance for gaming and professional workloads. - [Price: Rs.50,000]
