ABOUT NVIDIA

NVIDIA, a global tech leader, excels in GPUs for gaming and professional graphics. Their CUDA platform is pivotal for AI and scientific applications. Beyond graphics, NVIDIA contributes significantly to autonomous vehicles and data centers.

Some Popular Nvidia Series

GeForce GTX Series: Known for high-performance gaming graphics cards.

Quadro Series: Designed for professional graphics and content creation.

Titan Series: Blending gaming and professional capabilities for enthusiasts.

RTX Series: Featuring real-time ray tracing for enhanced visual realism.

NVIDIA A100 Tensor Core GPU: Designed for artificial intelligence and high-performance computing applications.
